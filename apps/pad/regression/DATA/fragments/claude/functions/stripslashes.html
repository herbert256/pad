The stripslashes function removes backslashes (PHP stripslashes).

Basic Usage 

{"it\\'s here" | stripslashes}

Result: "it\\'s here" | stripslashes

Clean Escaped Data 

{'say \\"hello\\"' | stripslashes}

Result: 'say \\"hello\\"' | stripslashes

Chained Examples 

{"John\\'s" | stripslashes | upper}

Unescaped and upper: "John\\'s" | stripslashes | upper

Round Trip 

{"it's here" | slashes}

With slashes: "it's here" | slashes 

{"it's here" | slashes | stripslashes}

Restored: "it's here" | slashes | stripslashes
