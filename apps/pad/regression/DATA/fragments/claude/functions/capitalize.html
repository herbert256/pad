The capitalize function capitalizes the first letter of each word.

Basic Usage 

{'hello world' | capitalize}

Result: 'hello world' | capitalize

From Lowercase 

{'JOHN DOE' | lower | capitalize}

Fixed caps: 'JOHN DOE' | lower | capitalize

Chained Examples 

{$sampleEmail | before('@') | replace('.', ' ') | capitalize}

Full name from email: John Doe 

{'the quick brown fox' | capitalize | left(13)}...

Title preview: 'the quick brown fox' | capitalize | left(13)...

With Users 

{users}
  {$email | before('@') | replace('.', ' ') | capitalize}
{/users}

Names from emails:

Alice
Bob
Charlie

Complex Chain 

{'product_category_name' | replace('_', ' ') | capitalize}

From snake_case: 'product_category_name' | replace('_', ' ') | capitalize
