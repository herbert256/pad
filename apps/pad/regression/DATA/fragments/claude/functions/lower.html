The lower function converts a string to lowercase.

Basic Usage 

{echo 'HELLO WORLD' | lower}

Result: hello world

Email Normalization 

{echo 'User@EXAMPLE.COM' | lower}

Normalized: user@example.com

Mixed Case 

{echo 'HeLLo WoRLd' | lower}

All lower: hello world

Chained Examples 

{echo $email | lower | before('@')}

Lowercase username: john.smith 

{echo 'MY FILE.TXT' | lower | replace(' ', '_')}

Normalized filename: my_file.txt 

{echo 'HELLO WORLD' | lower | capitalize}

Title case: Hello World

Note

Works with UTF-8 characters.
