The mid function extracts a middle substring.

Basic Usage

{echo 'Hello World' | mid(3, 5)}

Middle: llo W

Extract Section

{echo 'abcdefghij' | mid(2, 4)}

Chars 3-6: bcde

Single Character

{echo 'Hello' | mid(2, 1)}

Third char: e

Chained Examples

{echo $sampleText | mid(5, 10) | upper}

Middle section: O WORLD EX

{echo 'john.smith@example.com' | mid(0, 10) | replace('.', ' ') | capitalize}

Name portion: M

{echo '2024-12-15' | mid(5, 2)}

Month: -1

Note

Parameters: mid(start, length). Zero-indexed.
