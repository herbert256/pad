The replace function replaces all occurrences of a search string with a replacement.

Basic Usage 

{'hello world' | replace('world', 'there')}

Result: 'hello world' | replace('world', 'there')

Multiple Replacements 

{'a-b-c' | replace('-', '_') | replace('_', ' ')}

Chain replacements: 'a-b-c' | replace('-', '_') | replace('_', ' ')

URL Slugs 

{'Hello World Example' | lower | replace(' ', '-')}

URL slug: 'Hello World Example' | lower | replace(' ', '-')

Format Conversions 

{'snake_case_name' | replace('_', ' ') | capitalize}

Snake to title: 'snake_case_name' | replace('_', ' ') | capitalize 

{'kebab-case-name' | replace('-', ' ') | capitalize}

Kebab to title: 'kebab-case-name' | replace('-', ' ') | capitalize

Complex Chain 

{'2024-12-14' | replace('-', '/')}

Date format: '2024-12-14' | replace('-', '/') 

{$sampleEmail | replace('.', ' ') | replace('@', ' at ') | capitalize}

Readable email: John Doe At Example Com
