The bold function wraps the value in <b> tags.

Basic Usage 

{'important' | bold}

Result: 'important' | bold

Chained Examples 

{'hello world' | upper | bold}

Result: 'hello world' | upper | bold 

{'  warning  ' | trim | bold}

Result: ' warning ' | trim | bold

With Users 

{users}
  {$name | bold}: {$email}
{/users}

Bold names:

<b>Alice Johnson</b>: alice@example.com
<b>Bob Smith</b>: bob@example.com
<b>Charlie Brown</b>: charlie@example.com

Complex Chain 

{'john.doe' | replace('.', ' ') | capitalize | bold}

Result: 'john.doe' | replace('.', ' ') | capitalize | bold
