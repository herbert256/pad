The nbsp function converts spaces to non-breaking spaces (&nbsp;).

Basic Usage 

{echo 'Hello World' | nbsp}

Result: Hello World

Prevent Wrapping 

{echo '10 USD' | nbsp}

Price: 10 USD

Chained Examples 

{echo 'New York City' | nbsp | bold}

City: New York City 

{echo '100 km' | trim | nbsp}

Distance: 100 km

Note

Use to prevent line breaks between words that should stay together.
