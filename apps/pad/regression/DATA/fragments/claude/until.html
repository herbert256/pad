The until tag loops until a condition becomes true.

Basic Until Loop 

{set $i = 1}
{until $i gt 5}
  Item {$i}
  {increment $i}
{/until}

Until vs While Comparison

while $i le 5 = loop while i <= 5 (same as until $i gt 5)

Until with Counter 

{set $n = 0}
{until $n eq 10}
  {$n}
  {set $n = $n + 2}
{/until}
