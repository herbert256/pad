The white function normalizes whitespace - collapses multiple spaces to a single space.

Basic Usage 

{'hello    world' | white}

Result: 'hello world' | white

Clean User Input 

{'  multiple   spaces   here  ' | trim | white}

Cleaned: ' multiple spaces here ' | trim | white

Chained Examples 

{'  HELLO    WORLD  ' | trim | white | lower | capitalize}

Full cleanup: ' HELLO WORLD ' | trim | white | lower | capitalize 

{'John     Doe' | white | before(' ') | left(1)}. {'John     Doe' | white | after(' ')}

Abbreviated: 'John Doe' | white | before(' ') | left(1). 'John Doe' | white | after(' ')

Complex Chain 

{'Product:    Widget    XL    Blue' | white | after(': ') | replace(' ', '-') | lower}

Product slug: 'Product: Widget XL Blue' | white | after(': ') | replace(' ', '-') | lower
