The upper function converts a string to uppercase.

Basic Usage

{echo $sampleText | upper}

Result: HELLO WORLD EXAMPLE TEXT

Simple Text

{echo 'hello world' | upper}

Uppercase: HELLO WORLD

Mixed Case

{echo 'HeLLo WoRLd' | upper}

All upper: HELLO WORLD

Chained Examples

{echo $email | before('@') | upper}

Username uppercase: JOHN.SMITH

{echo $sampleText | left(10) | upper | trim}

First 10 upper: HELLO WORL

{echo 'hello' | upper | left(1)}

First initial: H

Note

Works with UTF-8 characters.
