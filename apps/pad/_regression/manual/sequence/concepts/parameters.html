Parameters

plays/actions can be added as a parameter to a seq tag,
but naming conflicts are possible and then above option way must be used.

HTML RESULT 
{sequence '10..20', name='mySeq'}
 
{sequence mySeq, even} {$sequence} {/sequence}
 10 12 12 14 14 16 16 18 18 20 20 
{sequence mySeq, shuffle} {$sequence} {/sequence}
 12 16 11 13 14 10 15 20 19 18 17 
{sequence mySeq, average} {$sequence} {/sequence}
 15
