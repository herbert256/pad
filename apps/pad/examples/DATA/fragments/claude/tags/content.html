The content tag defines reusable content blocks.

Basic Content Block

{content 'greeting'}
  <p>Hello World!</p>
{/content}

{greeting}

Rendered:

Hello World!

Content with Placeholder

{content 'wrapper'}
  <div style="border:1px solid #ccc; padding:10px;">
    @content@
  </div>
{/content}

{wrapper}
  This is the inner content
{/wrapper}
This is the inner content

Content with Variables

{set $userName = 'Alice'}
{content 'userBox'}
  <b>User: {$userName}</b>
{/content}

{userBox}
User: Alice
