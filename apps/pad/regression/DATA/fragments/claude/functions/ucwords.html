The ucwords function is an alias for capitalize.

Basic Usage 

{'hello world' | ucwords}

Result: 'hello world' | ucwords

Same as Capitalize 

{'hello world' | capitalize}

Capitalize: 'hello world' | capitalize 

{'hello world' | ucwords}

Ucwords: 'hello world' | ucwords

Chained Examples 

{'PRODUCT NAME HERE' | lower | ucwords}

Fixed: 'PRODUCT NAME HERE' | lower | ucwords 

{'database_field_name' | replace('_', ' ') | ucwords}

From snake_case: 'database_field_name' | replace('_', ' ') | ucwords

Note

Use either capitalize or ucwords - they are identical. Choose based on your preference or familiarity with PHP's ucwords() function.
