The now function returns the current Unix timestamp.

Basic Usage 

{now}

Current timestamp: 1765746360

Format with Date 

{now | date('Y-m-d')}

Today: 2025-12-14 

{now | date('H:i:s')}

Current time: 21:06:00

Chained Examples 

{now | date('Y')}

Current year: 2025 

{now | date('F j, Y')}

Full date: December 14, 2025 

{now | date('l')}

Day of week: Sunday

Calculations 

{now | date('Y') | + 1}

Next year: 2026 

{now | date('m') | + 0}

Current month number: 12
