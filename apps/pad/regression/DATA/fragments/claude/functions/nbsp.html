The nbsp function replaces spaces with &nbsp; (non-breaking spaces).

Basic Usage 

{'hello world' | nbsp}

Result: 'hello world' | nbsp

Prevent Line Breaks 

{'John Doe' | nbsp}

Won't break: 'John Doe' | nbsp 

{'New York' | nbsp}

City name: 'New York' | nbsp

Chained Examples 

{'  multiple spaces  ' | trim | white | nbsp}

Cleaned with nbsp: ' multiple spaces ' | trim | white | nbsp

Phone Numbers 

{'555 123 4567' | nbsp}

No break: '555 123 4567' | nbsp

With Users 

{users}
  {$name | nbsp}
{/users}

Names that won't break:

[Alice Johnson]
[Bob Smith]
[Charlie Brown]
