The continue tag transforms a stored sequence.

Store and Transform Sequence 

{sequence 5, push='mySeq'}
{continue add=10}
{pull:mySeq} {$sequence} {/pull:mySeq}

Original 1-5, after adding 10:

11 12 13 14 15

Multiple Transformations 

{sequence 5, push='nums'}
{continue add=100}
{continue reverse}
{pull:nums} {$sequence} {/pull:nums}

After add=100 and reverse:

105 104 103 102 101

Note

Use pull: prefix to avoid naming conflicts with application tags. The continue tag operates on a stored sequence (via push='name') and applies transformations like add, subtract, reverse, even, odd, etc.
