The echo tag evaluates and outputs expressions.

Basic Arithmetic 

{echo 5 + 3}
{echo 10 * 4}
{echo 100 / 5}
{echo 17 - 8}
  • 5 + 3 = 8
  • 10 * 4 = 40
  • 100 / 5 = 20
  • 17 - 8 = 9

With Variables 

{set $price = 25}
{set $quantity = 4}
Total: {echo $price * $quantity}

Total: 100

Complex Expressions 

{echo (10 + 5) * 2}
{echo 100 - (20 * 3)}
  • (10 + 5) * 2 = 30
  • 100 - (20 * 3) = 40

Pipe Functions with Echo 

{set $value = 3.14159}
{echo $value | %.2f}

Formatted: 3.14
