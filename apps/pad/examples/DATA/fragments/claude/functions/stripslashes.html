The stripslashes function removes backslashes from a string.

Basic Usage

{echo $slashedText | stripslashes}

Result: It's a "test"

Remove Escapes

{echo 'It\'s a \"test\"' | stripslashes}

Cleaned: It's a "test"

Chained Examples

{echo 'He said \"hello\"' | stripslashes | upper}

Clean and upper: HE SAID "HELLO"

Note

Opposite of slashes. Use to clean escaped strings.
