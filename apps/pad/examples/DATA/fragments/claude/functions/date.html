The date function formats a timestamp using PHP date format.

Basic Usage 

{echo {now} | date('Y-m-d')}

Today: 2025-12-17

Common Formats 

{echo {now} | date('d/m/Y')}

European: 17/12/2025 

{echo {now} | date('M j, Y')}

Readable: Dec 17, 2025 

{echo {now} | date('l')}

Day name: Wednesday

With Time 

{echo {now} | date('Y-m-d H:i:s')}

Full: 2025-12-17 06:01:33

With Offset 

{echo {now} | date('Y-m-d', '+1 week')}

Next week: 2025-12-24 

{echo {now} | date('Y-m-d', '-1 month')}

Last month: 2025-11-17

Format Codes

  • Y - 4-digit year
  • m - Month (01-12)
  • d - Day (01-31)
  • H - Hour (00-23)
  • i - Minutes (00-59)
  • s - Seconds (00-59)
  • l - Full day name
  • F - Full month name
