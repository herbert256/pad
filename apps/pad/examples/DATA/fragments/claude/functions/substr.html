The substr function extracts a substring by position and length.

Basic Usage 

{echo $sampleText | substr(0, 5)}

First 5 chars: Hello

From Position 

{echo 'Hello World' | substr(6)}

From position 6: World

Negative Index 

{echo 'Hello World' | substr(-5)}

Last 5 chars: World

Chained Examples 

{echo $sampleText | substr(0, 10) | upper}

First 10 uppercase: HELLO WORL 

{echo 'abcdefghij' | substr(2, 4) | upper}

Middle portion: CDEF 

{echo $email | substr(0, 3) | upper}

First 3 of email: JOH

Note

Parameters: substr(start, length). Negative start counts from end.
