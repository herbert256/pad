The cut function removes all occurrences of a text (replace with empty).

Basic Usage 

{'hello world' | cut('o')}

Result: 'hello world' | cut('o')

Remove Separators 

{'123-456-7890' | cut('-')}

Phone digits only: '123-456-7890' | cut('-') 

{'$1,234.56' | cut('$') | cut(',')}

Clean number: '$1,234.56' | cut('$') | cut(',')

Chained Examples 

{'  hello   world  ' | trim | white | cut(' ')}

Remove all spaces: ' hello world ' | trim | white | cut(' ') 

{'user@example.com' | cut('@') | cut('.')}

Letters only: 'user@example.com' | cut('@') | cut('.')

Complex Chain 

{'ISBN: 978-3-16-148410-0' | after(': ') | cut('-')}

ISBN digits: 'ISBN: 978-3-16-148410-0' | after(': ') | cut('-') 

{'(555) 123-4567' | cut('(') | cut(')') | cut(' ') | cut('-')}

Phone digits: '(555) 123-4567' | cut('(') | cut(')') | cut(' ') | cut('-')
