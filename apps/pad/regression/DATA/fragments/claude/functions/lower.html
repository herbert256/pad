The lower function converts text to lowercase.

Basic Usage 

{'HELLO WORLD' | lower}

Result: 'HELLO WORLD' | lower

Normalize Input 

{'JoHn.DoE@EXAMPLE.COM' | lower}

Email normalized: 'JoHn.DoE@EXAMPLE.COM' | lower

Chained Examples 

{'FILE.TXT' | lower | before('.')}

Lowercase filename without extension: 'FILE.TXT' | lower | before('.') 

{'  HELLO WORLD  ' | trim | lower | capitalize}

Clean and title case: ' HELLO WORLD ' | trim | lower | capitalize

URL Slugs 

{'My Blog Post Title' | lower | replace(' ', '-')}

URL slug: 'My Blog Post Title' | lower | replace(' ', '-')

Complex Chain 

{'USER: JOHN_DOE' | after(': ') | lower | replace('_', ' ') | capitalize}

Format username: 'USER: JOHN_DOE' | after(': ') | lower | replace('_', ' ') | capitalize
