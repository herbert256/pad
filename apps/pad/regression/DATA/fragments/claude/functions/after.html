The after function returns everything after the first occurrence of a delimiter.

Basic Usage 

{$samplePath | after('/')}

Path: /home/user/documents/file.txt

After first /: home/user/documents/file.txt

Email Domain 

{$sampleEmail | after('@')}

Email: john.doe@example.com

Domain: example.com

Chained with Other Functions 

{$sampleEmail | after('@') | before('.') | upper}

Domain name only, uppercase: EXAMPLE 

{$samplePath | after('/') | after('/') | after('/')}

Filename from path: documents/file.txt

Complex Chain 

{'user:john:admin:active' | after(':') | before(':') | capitalize}

Extract username: 'user:john:admin:active' | after(':') | before(':') | capitalize
