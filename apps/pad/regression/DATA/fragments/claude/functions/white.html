The white function normalizes whitespace (collapses multiple spaces to single space).

Basic Usage 

{echo $spacedText | white}

Result: lots of spaces

Multiple Spaces 

{echo 'too    many     spaces' | white}

Normalized: too many spaces

Mixed Whitespace 

{echo 'tabs  and   spaces' | white}

Cleaned: tabs and spaces

Chained Examples 

{echo $spacedText | white | trim}

Full cleanup: lots of spaces 

{echo '  hello    world  ' | white | trim | capitalize}

Clean and format: Hello World 

{echo 'Name:    John    Doe' | white | after(': ')}

Extract value: John Doe

Note

Combine with trim for full whitespace normalization.
