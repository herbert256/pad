The time function formats a timestamp as time only.

Basic Usage 

{echo {now} | time}

Current time: 2025-12-20

Custom Format 

{echo {now} | time('H:i')}

24-hour: 14:12 

{echo {now} | time('h:i A')}

12-hour: 02:12 PM

With Seconds 

{echo {now} | time('H:i:s')}

With seconds: 14:12:54

Chained Examples 

{echo {now} | time('H:i') | bold}

Bold time: 14:12

Note

Shortcut for time-only formatting. Use date for full date-time formatting.
