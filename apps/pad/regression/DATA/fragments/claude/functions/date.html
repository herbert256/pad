The date function formats a timestamp using PHP date format.

Basic Usage 

{$sampleTimestamp | date('Y-m-d')}

Formatted: 2025-12-14

Common Formats 

{now | date('Y-m-d')}

ISO date: 2025-12-14 

{now | date('d/m/Y')}

European: 14/12/2025 

{now | date('m/d/Y')}

US format: 12/14/2025 

{now | date('F j, Y')}

Long format: December 14, 2025 

{now | date('D, M j')}

Short format: Sun, Dec 14

Time Formats 

{now | date('H:i:s')}

24-hour time: 21:05:59 

{now | date('g:i A')}

12-hour time: 9:05 PM

With Modifier 

{now | date('Y-m-d', '+1 week')}

Next week: 2025-12-21 

{now | date('Y-m-d', '-1 month')}

Last month: 2025-11-14 

{now | date('F Y', '+6 months')}

In 6 months: June 2026

Chained Example 

{now | date('Y') | + 0} ©
