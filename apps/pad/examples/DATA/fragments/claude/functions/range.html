The range function tests if a value is within a range (inclusive).

Basic Usage

{if {echo 5 | range(1, 10)}}In range{/if}

Result: 5 is in range 1-10

Inclusive Boundaries

{if {echo 10 | range(1, 10)}}At boundary{/if}

Edge: 10 IS in range 1-10 (inclusive)

Outside Range

{if not {echo 15 | range(1, 10)}}Out of range{/if}

Result: 15 is NOT in range 1-10

Age Validation

{set $age = 25}
{if {echo $age | range(18, 65)}}Working age{/if}

Age: 25 is working age (18-65)

Note

Inclusive: boundaries ARE included. Use between for exclusive comparison.
